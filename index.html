FULL BIKE SERVICE

Iron Cycles knows how important bike maintenance is
So servicing your Harley-Davidson at Iron Cycles will keep your bike running at it's BEST

Our service mechanics perform all factory recommended 2500 mile interval maintenance procedures, which include:

 
  • Drain engine oil & filter and replace with Dynacycle Oil
    & Oil Filter *
  • Clean or replace tappet screen (Big Twin only)
  • Drain primary lube and replace with bike specified type
  • Adjust primary chain
  • Adjust clutch
  • Inspect & clean spark plugs
  • Clean or replace air filter
  • Check tire condition and air pressure
  • Check brake fluid
  • Check condition of front and rear brakes
  • Check charging system & battery
  • Check all electrical components and functions
  • Adjust & lube (throttle, idle, & clutch) cables
  • Adjust and check condition of drive chain or belt
  • Check suspension
  • Test ride
  • Check for oil leaks
 
 

ADDITIONAL MAINTENANCE EVERY 10K MILES

Drain fork oil and replace with PJ1 Fork Oil

Drain transmission lube and replace with DynaCycle* (Big Twin only)

Lube and adjust neck bearings
 

* We strongly recommend using DynaCycle for all your Oil and Transmission needs over any other manufactures brand oils.
